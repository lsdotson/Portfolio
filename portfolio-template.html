Get in Touch

Vlocity is a leading provider of custom Salesforce platforms for companies like T-Mobile, Blue Shield, and New York Life.

Vlocity enlisted us to design a fresh take on consumer insurance: a chat-based mobile app that allows users to easily manage their policies, purchase microinsurance, and get relevant, timely coverage recommendations.

We kicked off with two hours of ideation. We wanted the app to be primarily chat-driven, with relevant notifications, easy policy management, and a consumer-friendly feel. We also wanted to highlight Vlocity's new embedded form capabilities that allow users to fill out microforms without leaving chat.

We assessed our concepts, ran them by the studio, and moved into Sketch to begin fleshing out the details. At the same time, Emma explored visual directions, names, and logos (because this product will be white-labeled, we had a lot of freedom here).
